StruggleBus

This solid propellent rocket competed in the 2022 FAR-51025 Competition for the 10k altitude category.

Overview

Motor Type:

commercial solid


Body Material:

fiberglass


Height:

87 inches


Payload:

Live camera, GPS

About Strugglebus

The structure and original avionics of this rocket were built in 2019, but the build was followed by a year of failed separation tests that earned it the moniker “Strugglebus”. Then Covid hit just as we nailed down separation tests, grounding the poor rocket for another year and a half. Finally, after a redesign of the avionics and motor mounting, Strugglebus had a perfect first flight at the FAR 51025 competition in June 2022!

Launch History

June 2022 @ FAR 51025, 8761 feet, live video streaming payload
